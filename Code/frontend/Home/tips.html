How Rule Two Works

This rule says that you can think about retirement
when you have funds worth 25 times your annual
expenses.

How Rule Three Works

Get excited about saving.
Use the Rule of 72 to quickly calculate how many years it’ll
take to double your money. (Divide 72 by the interest rate. )

How Rule Four Works

The maximum loan you can take should not eat up more
than 40% of your net monthly income.

How Rule Five Works

Before you invest, make sure you have an emergency fund.
Your emergency fund should be equal to 6x your monthly
income.

How Rule Six Works

The best way to create wealth is to pay yourself first.Invest 10-20% of
your income and always put it aside in the first week of the month.
